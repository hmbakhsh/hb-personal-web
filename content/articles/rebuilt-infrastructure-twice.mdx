Sometimes you have to tear things down to build them right. Here's the story of rebuilding our infrastructure twice in six months.

## The First Rebuild

Started with a monolith. It worked, until it didn't. Traffic grew, complexity grew, and suddenly deploys took 45 minutes.

## The Second Rebuild

Microservices seemed like the answer. Spoiler: they weren't. At least not for a team of three.

## What Actually Worked

The hybrid approach. Keep the monolith for core logic, extract only what truly needs to scale independently.
