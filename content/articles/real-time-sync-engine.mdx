Real-time sync is deceptively complex. Here's how we built one that actually works.

## The Challenge

Multiple users editing the same data simultaneously. Offline support. Conflict resolution. All while keeping the UI snappy.

## Our Approach

We use operational transforms combined with a CRDT-like structure for specific data types. It's not pure CRDT, but it gets the job done.
